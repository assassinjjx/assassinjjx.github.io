1.使用类图，对Asg_RH文档中Make Reservation用例开展领域建模

2.使用类图，对Asg_RH文档中Payment用例开展领域建模

3.根据上述模型，给出建议的数据表以及主要字段，特别是主键和外键

  • 注意事项：

    • 对象必须是名词、特别是技术名词、报表、描述类的处理；
    • 关联必须有多重性、部分有名称与导航方向
    • 属性要注意计算字段
    • 数据建模，为了简化描述仅需要给出表清单，例如：Hotel（ID/Key，Name，LoctionID/Fkey，Address…..）

  • Hotel(ID/Key，Name，CityID/Fkey，Address, Star Rate,Lowest Price, Type)

  • Room(ID/Key, Type)
  • City(ID/Key, Name, Type)
  • Customer(ID/Key, Name, Email)
  • Reservation(ID/Key, CustomerID/Fkey, HotelID/Fkey, CheckInDate, CheckOutDate)
  • RoomReservationInfo(ID/Key, ReservationID/Fkey, Adult Number, Children Number,Children Age)
  • CreditCard(ID/Key, CustomerID/Fkey, Bank ID/Fkey, Card Number, Card Security Code, Card Password, Type)
  • Bank(ID/Key, Name)
  • Empower(ID/key, Bank ID/Fkey, CustomerID/Fkey, Publisher, User)
  • Payment(ID/Key, ReservationID/Fkey)
  • Form(ID/Key, PaymentID/Fkey, Card Number,Card Security Code, Card Password, Name, Address, City, Country, Postcode, Telephone)
  • Confirmation(ID/Key, PaymentID/Fkey)
